The Experience

How Our Tastings Work

Every week we curate a themed flight — sometimes a storied region, a single producer, a varietal deep-dive, or a creative theme. Walk in and let us guide you.

01

Walk In

No reservation needed. Visit our tasting counter Thursday through Sunday during tasting hours.

02

Meet the Flight

3–4 wines tied by a narrative — region, producer, varietal, or theme. Each pour is 2 oz, served one at a time.

03

Learn as You Sip

Our team walks you through each wine: region, producer, what to look for — guided but never stuffy.

04

Take a Bottle Home

Love something? Purchase any bottle and open it on the spot — no corkage fee. By-the-glass list rotates weekly too.
